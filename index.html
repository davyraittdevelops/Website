About Me

I'm Davy, an engineer passionate about building cloud-native software on AWS. I have experience in mobile development using Native Android, Flutter, React-native, and backend development with Python and Typescript. My expertise also includes DevOps practices like CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and performance monitoring.
After completing an MBO in Network and System Administration at Zoomvliet College Roosendaal from 2013 to 2017, I began my career managing network systems for an elder care company, gaining valuable experience in IT infrastructure over two years.
Eager to deepen my expertise, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at Avans Hogeschool in Breda, where I studied from 2019 to 2023. During my studies, I specialized in a cloud-native software minor focused on AWS and undertook a notable project developing blockchain applications for the Openbaar Ministerie. Additionally, I completed an internship at ihomer, where I developed a mobile healthcare app, applying my learning in a real-world setting. I graduated cum laude, reflecting my commitment to my educational pursuits.
After graduation, I began freelancing in full-stack, cloud, and mobile development. This work allowed me to apply my skills on diverse projects worldwide, further expanding my practical experience across different sectors.
In December 2021, I joined Loyally as a Software Engineer, where I was involved in full-stack development, including integrating CRM systems and migrating applications to Azure. My role heavily utilized CI/CD pipelines and cloud technologies, preparing me for advanced technical challenges. Currently, I work as a Cloud/DevOps Engineer at Essent, the Netherlands' largest energy company, a role I have held since February 2024. Here, I am pivotal in deploying and managing cloud-native solutions, ensuring our IT infrastructure is scalable and efficient. This position represents the ongoing evolution of my career in technology, where I continually strive to leverage and enhance my capabilities in a dynamic industry landscape.
